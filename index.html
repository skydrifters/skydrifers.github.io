A Social Group

We have an active member base which holds occasional social nights, a get together where everyone jumps on our Discord server and we generally have a good time talking about Worlds Adrift News, games and we ask the mods awkward questions about the political situation. We pride ourselves in being a social group with every member being able to do what they want in the game.

person_pin

Based on freedom

Although we pride ourselves on being a social group we believe that any member has the right to what they want in the game. We all do our own thing in a sense, while helping out each other whenever we can; we are more of a group of individuals that fly under one banner. But contribution is key, you take as much as you can give. We aim to bring about harmony to WA, help those that need it and make the world as pleasant as possible.

Allied, hence United

We are a founding faction of the Free Skies Coallition. A group of alliances, such as The Harbourmen, Orchestra and the Aviator Innovators. Which pride themselves in ensuring the smaller Alliances have support, protection and aid from larger tyrannical groups. Also to promote each players rights and freedom in game. [WIP, needs more]

Testimonials, from Chief Dayox

"We are one of the oldest factions of Worlds Adrift, being one of a few Precursor factions; a faction that was founded before the Alliance Integration on the Website. We’re also pretty big if I do say so myself with I believe 156 members under the name of Sky Drifter."
"We occasionally host Social Nights where we jump onto discord, discuss WA news, play games and generally have a good time!"
"The Sky Drifters are not your typical faction, we all do our own thing in a sense, all pursue our own goals while helping out each other whenever we can. The Sky Drifters are more of a group of individuals that fly under one banner, we aim to bring about harmony to WA, help those that need it and make the world as pleasant as possible."
"We have a Sister Faction, WANMED who focus on being our more combat ready ships and crews, often being the Vanguard of the fleet, led by Captain Ryouji."
"While the Drifters aren’t the warring type, they are always prepared to answer a call to arms. Yet this does not mean they are hostile to those that approach them, in fact you’ll fine they’re more than happy to lend a hand to those that require it."
"If the Drifters intrigue you, then do not hesitate to approach them, they’re always looking to for members, just ask for Dayox and he’s often on hand to welcome new citizens to the ranks."

Led by 1st Chief Dayox and Commanders Hawkeye and Razordor
