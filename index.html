A Social Group

We have an active member base which holds regular social nights, a get together where everyone jumps on our Discord server and we generally have a good time talking about Worlds Adrift News, games and we ask the mods awkward questions about the political situation. We pride ourselves in being a social group with every member being able to do what they want in the game.

person_pin

Based on freedom

Although we pride ourselves on being a social group we believe that any member has the right to what they want in the game. We all do our own thing in a sense, while helping out each other whenever we can; we are more of a group of individuals that fly under one banner. But contribution is key, you take as much as you can give. We aim to bring about harmony to WA, help those that need it and make the world as pleasant as possible.

The. Best.

We have Human Leadership also known as "Chief Dayox" who isn't afraid to come down from Chiefdom and talk to his alliance and interact with us. Every opinion is vaild at the boardroom table and all have the right to voice such opinions to the members. But as I said we have human leadership so people will disagree with you, that is just part of being a drifter.

Who we are: from Chief Dayox

....in his pirate voice

"We are one of the oldest factions of Worlds Adrift… One of the few Precursor factions, founded in the days of yore, a time before Alliances were even established. When hostilities and handshakes existed side-by-side amidst the tavern-like forums of yesteryear…"
"But alas… the good ole days…. Be much better now!"
"Now we sport well over 150 deck swabbers happy to don the name o’ ‘Sky Drifter’"
"We’re a motley crew of scallywags who gather as best we can fer (ir)regular Social Nights out on the Airwaves… ruff housing in Discord(ant) jubilee, talkin news (certainly not gossip), and generally havin’ a jolly ole time!"
"We Drifters aint yer typical faction. We all bugger off to our own devices n’ ‘ave our own goals, yet still maintain a semblance of camaraderie… lending a hand, a peg-leg, or a head to help our fellow Drifter whenever n’ wherever we can. Like the sovereign clouds n’ islands drifting under one sky; we be a group of individuals flying under one banner!"
"We aim to to bring about harmony to the shattered lands of Worlds Adrift. We help those in need and work to make the world as pleasant as possible."
"But think not we weak! Ye gunslinging rabble-rousers n’ Cannon Bricks best beware!! Som' our members be h'ppy in good fight! n can be found t' thirst for the blood of sinners!!"
"We Drifters aren’t warring savages, but we bare our fangs n’ answer our brother’s Call to Arms if barbarians show themselves. We’re always happy to lend a hooked hand or ’haps even offer a place on our ships, should ye be stranded."
"If our sorry lot o’ righteous basterds interests ye kind heart, dont think twice! Or do! Look ye fer ‘Dayox’ our Commander in Chief’s Underwear. He be swabbing decks n piloting ships alike. Ye can find him all across the Discord and forum-taverns of World’s Adrift, always ready to welcome ye good citizens-o-the-sky to our ranks."

Commanded by Chief Dayox
